In the upcoming final installment of this series, we will address the most common question for growing AWS environments: Should I use VPC Peering or Transit Gateway?

While VPC Peering is highly performant and has zero data processing costs, it creates a "Full Mesh" nightmare as you grow. Transit Gateway simplifies management but introduces a processing fee. We'll look at the "Point of No Return" where TGW becomes mandatory, and how to use both together for a hybrid approach.
